# Support

Questions, ideas, and "is this a bug?" are all welcome.

- **Something's wrong, or a feature request** — open an issue using the
  templates in the relevant [emu198x](https://github.com/emu198x) repo.
- **Accuracy reports** — a divergence from real hardware or a failing test
  vector is a bug report; please include the system and the vector.
- **Security** — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md); please report privately.

It's a small, long-haul project, so replies may take a little while. Thanks for
your patience.
